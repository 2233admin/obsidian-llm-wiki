---
name: vault-emerge
description: Identify emerging patterns across recent notes
---

Find emerging patterns across notes from the last 14 days.

Steps:
1. Use `vault.search` and `vault.graph` to map recent note clusters
2. Look for: topics gaining momentum (mentioned more frequently over time), new connections forming between previously unlinked entities, concepts shifting meaning or scope, recurring questions that haven't been answered
3. Report findings as a structured list: pattern name, evidence (note links), strength (weak/forming/strong), suggested action (watch/synthesize/act)

Do not write any notes unless the user confirms a specific pattern is worth capturing.
